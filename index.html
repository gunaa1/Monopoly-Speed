Purpose

Sustainability Aspect

In the game of Monopoly, often small pieces, such as money (the cash) get lost. Due to this, many people dispose the entire game and buy a new set as they believe that they would not be able to play a proper game of Monopoly again. To help reduce this waste (sustainability), I built this website that compliments the money aspect of the game.

Simplicity Aspect

Another advantage of this website is that it helps people who are frustrated by the complexity of the money aspect in Monopoly have fun. This is due to the money aspect taking up a lot of time and also just being tiresome. This creates another set of audience that come here for a different reason than sustainability.

Rules

  1. Each Player starts with $1500.
  2. The banker keeps personal funds seperate from bank when less than 4 players. If 5 or more players, then a exclusive banker is chosen.
  3. Each player rolls. Player with highest number goes first and so on.
  4. If double is rolled, go again. If this happens thrice, then go to Jail.
  5. Each time GO is passed, a player is payed $200. If in debt, you only recieve $100.
  1. Players can buy properties for listed price.
  2. Players can buy properties during auctions too, for much less.
  3. If a player lands on other's property, they have to pay rent.
  4. Rent depends on the number of hotels set up on the property.
  5. Colored locations have to be sold back to bank only. Other locations can be sold in private transactions.
  6. Hotels maybe sold back to the bank for 1/2 the price at any point.
  7. A player can take a mortage, but has to pay back value + 10%. Additionally, they cannot buy other properties of same color while this mortage is existent.
  1. Draw a card when landing on the Chance or Community Chest spot.
  2. Hold GET OUT OF JAIL card for later use.
  3. In Income Tax, either pay $200 or 10% of your wealth.
  4. If player landed on or got a Go to Jail location/spot, go to Jail. Also a triple double can land a player in jail.
  5. Get out of jail by: rolling a double, paying $50 fine, using/purchasing (from another player) get out of jail, wait 3 turns.
  6. On Free Parking, nothing occurs.
  1. A player can take a maximum of $500 in debt.
  2. When you owe more than you can pay, you declare bankruptcy.
  3. All of you wealth goes to the person you are in debt with.
  4. If this is another player, then they get 50% of all money, and the properties go back to the bank.
